<p>Over the June Holidays, some of our Saints in the Talent Development Programme took part in the various competitions and we are proud to share their following achievements.</p>
<p>1) Singapore Futures Youth Competition<br />This competition was organised by the Lee Kuan Yew School of Public Policy and its theme was &ldquo;Re-imaging SG75&rdquo;. Tan Yuan Zong from 21S18 came in first with his team mates from other institutions while Martin Song from 21A04 came in second with his team. Well done!</p>
<p>2) Micron NUS-ISE Business Analytics Case Competition<br />SAJC was represented by 2 teams of 4 students each. The team comprising of Kelly Wang Sze Qing (21S10), Samuel Tan Qing Hui (21S10), Nguyen Thu Hoai (20S11) and Nguyen Ngoc Bao Han (21S18) garnered the 2nd runner up position. We also wished to acknowledge the effort put in by Phung Nguyen Hanh (20S12), Steve Sutantio (21S11), Amelie Grace Hartadi (21S04) and Nguyen Phuong Thao (21S11). Up and on!</p>
