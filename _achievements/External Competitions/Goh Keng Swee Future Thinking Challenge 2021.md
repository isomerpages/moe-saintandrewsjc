<p>3 of our JC1 students took part in the Goh Keng Swee Future Thinking Challenge, which required participants to come up with ideas to tackle the theme &ldquo;The Future of National Security Challenges for Singapore in a Post COVID-19 world.&rdquo; The students are Martin Song from 21A04, Nathalie Yeo from 21A04 and Ajay Navin Selven from 21S21. We are pleased to announce that they were awarded with the Commendation Award for their efforts. Well done and congratulations!</p>
