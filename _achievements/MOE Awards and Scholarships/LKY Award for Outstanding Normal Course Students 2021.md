<p>The Lee Kuan Yew Award for Outstanding Normal Course Students was presented for the first time in 2004. This award aims to recognise students in the Normal Academic and Normal Technical course for their academic achievements, as well as to encourage them to continue their academic accomplishments.</p>
<p>We are proud to share that Ashleigh Ng from 21A06 is one of the ten recipients this year to receive this prestigious award. Well done and congratulations to Ashleigh!</p>
