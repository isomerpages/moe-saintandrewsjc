<p>The Harvard Alumni for Global Women's Empowerment Essay Contest is organised
by the Harvard Alumni Association for JC1 students to participate in. Students
are required to write a 750-word essay on the question, What is the biggest
challenge facing women and girls in your country today?</p>
<p>Out of 19 participants from SAJC, we are pleased to share that 5 of them
have clinched an award each.</p>
