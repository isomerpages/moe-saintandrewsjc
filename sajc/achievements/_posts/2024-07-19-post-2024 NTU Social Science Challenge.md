<p>NTU Social Science Challenge is a competition for pre-university students
to foster innovative solutions to real-world policy challenges affecting
4 groups in Singapore. They are low-income families, single parents, migrant
workers and youths at risk. 3 groups of Saints participated in this and
we are pleased to announce that 1 of the groups clinched 2nd place in this
inaugural challenge. The group comprises of the following students.</p>
<p>1) Mah Jia May 23A04</p>
